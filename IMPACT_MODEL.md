# MeetMind — Impact Model

## The Business Problem (Quantified)

- Average enterprise meeting: 5 attendees, 45 minutes = 3.75 person-hours consumed
- Average follow-up time per attendee: 15 minutes (task logging, Slack messages, reminders)
- Total follow-up burden per meeting: 5 × 15 min = 75 minutes = 1.25 person-hours
- Average Indian tech salary: ₹800/hour
- Cost of follow-up per meeting: 1.25 × ₹800 = ₹1,000
- Enterprise teams have ~4 such meetings per week
- Weekly follow-up cost per team: ₹4,000
- Annual follow-up cost per team: ₹2,08,000

## What MeetMind Saves

MeetMind automates:
- Task extraction (saves ~5 min per attendee)
- Owner assignment (saves ~3 min per attendee)
- Deadline tracking setup (saves ~2 min per attendee)
- Escalation drafting (saves ~5 min per at-risk task)
- Meeting summary writing (saves ~10 min per meeting)

Conservative estimate: 70% reduction in follow-up time.

## Per-Meeting ROI (Demo Numbers)

From our live demo run:
- Attendees: 5
- Duration: 52 minutes
- Person-hours consumed: 4.33
- Manual follow-up without MeetMind: 1.25 hours
- Follow-up saved with MeetMind (70%): 0.88 hours
- Value at ₹800/hr across 5 people: ₹3,520 per meeting

## Beyond Cost Saving

- Zero missed action items → fewer project delays
- Dependency visibility → blockers caught before they cascade
- Sentiment detection → manager intervention 48 hours earlier
- Audit trail → compliance and accountability built in
- SLA prediction → breach prevented, not just reported
